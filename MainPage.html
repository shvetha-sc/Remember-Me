Objective

This game intends on helping you memorize and recollect the things important to you.

How do I play

1.A question will be posed and you will be asked be asked to find a person amongst a set of pictures within a certain timeframe.

2.You will be given three chances to give a correct answer.

3.For every wrong answer you will loose a life.

4.You will gain a point for every right answer.

5.Click on the picture of the person corresponding to the question.
